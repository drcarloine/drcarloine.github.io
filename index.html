A Journey Through The Ear


Project designed for Perception in Multimedia Computing module.
It was an open assignment.
My aim was to create an educational app that would give adults a refresher on their knowledge of different parts of the ear.
Has a Tutorial mode that guides the user through the ear,
and a Quiz mode that tests their knowledge.
Made using openFrameworks.

Orpheus Ka Sangeet


Orpheus Ka Sangeet, a.k.a. The Live Music Box, is an interactive, user-dependent online experience which takes live webcam input and produces audio and visual outputs.
It was the final project of mine and Aanandita Gupta in the Introduction to Computational Arts Practice module.
The users are invited to play an instrument (guitar, drums or piano) by moving their body in front of their webcam.
When motion is detected in different sections of the output on the screen a corresponding sound is played.
The user can only see the parts of their body that is moving and it is represented in white but the user can also choose a different colour.
Created using p5.js, MaxiLib, MaxiExtras, HTML and CSS.
You can read more about it and test it out on this website: Orhpeus Ka Sangeet
Source code on GitLab: Orpheus Ka Sangeet
